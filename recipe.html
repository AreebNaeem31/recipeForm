Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and qiuck dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beatens egg cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Aproximately 10 minuts
  • Preparation: 5 minuts
  • cooking: 5 minuts

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to taste
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese,decided vegetables,herbs,cooked meats

Instructions

1.

Beat the eggs:In bowl,beat the egg with pinch of saltand pepper until theyare well mixed. You can add tablespoonof water and milk for a fluffier texture.

2.

Heat the pan:Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.

3.

Cook the Omelette:Once the butter is melting and bubbling,pour n the egg.Tilt the panto ensure the eggs evenlycoat the surface.

4.

Add Filling (optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.

5.

Fold and Serve:As the omelette to continues to Cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling.Let it cook for another minut, then slide it on plate.

6.

Enjoy:Serve hot and additonal salt and pepper if need.
